3.4 Nemotron Integration with Sentinel
The Nemotron model is embedded in Sentinel at three points. First, it generates customized opening hooks for each lead based on segment tag, property value, and service zone. Second, it constructs personalized objection responses during live calls when Atlas flags an unhandled objection. Third, it drafts follow-up email copy for the Day 7 welcome trigger and long-tail nurture sequences. For $5M+ leads, Nemotron drafts a one-page property brief for CEO review before Workflow 3 fires.

IV. MODULE B — SOCIAL MEDIA AUTOMATION
4.1 Module Overview
Module B automates the full lifecycle of a social media post from content brief to platform publication. Banana Pro AI generates visual assets. The Nemotron model drafts captions and platform-specific copy. Airtable serves as the single source of record. Buffer handles scheduled publishing to Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and Alignable. Zapier connects all components.

4.2 Content Pillars
Brand | Property oversight, storm prep, service zone coverage, inspection documentation
CEO Journey | Enterprise building, leadership, operational systems, market positioning

4.3 Airtable Content Calendar Schema
Post Date (Date) | Platform (Multi-select: Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Alignable) | Caption (Long text, Nemotron-drafted, CEO-reviewed) | Asset (Attachment, Banana Pro AI export) | Content Pillar (Single select: Brand / CEO Journey) | Status (Single select: Draft, Approved, Scheduled, Published) | AI Draft ID (Link to AI Log table) | Notes (Long text)

4.4 Workflow Sequence
1. CEO or team member submits content brief to Nanobanana in natural language.
2. Nanobanana routes brief to Nemotron. Nemotron drafts caption for each target platform.
3. Draft record created in Airtable Content Calendar with Status = Draft.
4. Slack alert fires to #ai-drafts with preview. CEO reviews in Airtable, attaches Banana Pro AI asset.
5. CEO changes Status to Approved. Zapier detects field change.
6. Zapier posts Slack confirmation to #content-calendar and pushes post to Buffer.
7. Zapier updates Airtable Status to Scheduled.
8. On Buffer publish confirmation, Zapier updates Status to Published.

4.5 Caption Generation Prompt
SYSTEM CONTEXT: You are the content voice for Coastal Key Treasure Coast Asset Management, providing institutional-grade home watch and property oversight on Florida's Treasure Coast. Clients: absentee homeowners, luxury owners, investors, seasonal residents. Tone: authoritative, concise, risk-aware. No exclamation points. No emojis unless specified. Close with risk or consequence frame.

INPUT: Platform (Instagram / Facebook / LinkedIn / Alignable) | Pillar (Brand / CEO Journey) | Brief (plain language) | Length (Short <150w / Medium 150-300w / Long 300+w) | CTA (Book inspection / Visit website / DM for assessment / No CTA)


V. MODULE C — CONTENT PRODUCTION ENGINE
5.1 Module Overview
Module C extends the automation stack to long-form content: short-form video scripts, podcast episode outlines and talking points, YouTube thumbnails and descriptions, and multi-platform repurposing. All content originates from a Nanobanana brief, passes through Nemotron for drafting, is reviewed by the CEO in Airtable, and is distributed through the existing publishing stack.

5.2 Content Production Types
Short-Form Video | IG Reels, FB Reels, YT Shorts, TikTok | 60-90s script + hook + CTA
Long-Form Video | YouTube, LinkedIn Video | 3-8 min script with timestamps and B-roll notes
Podcast Episode | RSS feed distribution | Outline, talking points, intro/outro, show notes
Repurposed Asset | All platforms | One source piece broken into 5-8 derivative assets
YouTube Optimization | YouTube | Title, description, tags, chapters, thumbnail brief

5.3 Video Script Generation Workflow
1. Submit video brief to Nanobanana: topic, target segment, platform, desired length, and CTA.
2. Nemotron generates full script including hook (first 3 seconds), body (problem/solution/proof), and close.
3. Script posted to Airtable Video Production table as Draft. Slack alert fires to #content-production.
4. CEO reviews and edits in Airtable. Status changed to Approved.
5. Approved script triggers Zapier workflow that assembles production brief: script, B-roll notes, thumbnail brief, and caption variants per platform.
6. Production brief posted to Airtable. Banana Pro AI generates visual assets from thumbnail brief.
7. On video completion, asset attached to Airtable record. Zapier pushes to Buffer for scheduled publish.

5.4 Podcast Episode Workflow
1. Submit podcast brief to Nanobanana: episode topic, target audience segment, approximate length, and guest context.
2. Nemotron generates episode outline, talking points per section, intro script, outro script, and show notes.
3. All outputs stored in Airtable Podcast Production table. Status = Draft.
4. CEO reviews in Airtable. Approves with Status change.
5. Zapier generates show notes post per platform and queues in Buffer. LinkedIn and Facebook receive long-form show notes. Instagram receives short-form clip teaser caption.

5.5 Airtable Video Production Table Schema
Episode / Video Title (Short text) | Content Type (Single select: Short-Form Video, Long-Form Video, Podcast, Repurposed) | Target Segment (Single select: Absentee, Luxury, Investor, Seasonal, STR) | Platform(s) (Multi-select) | Script (Long text, Nemotron-generated) | B-Roll Notes (Long text) | Thumbnail Brief (Long text, passed to Banana Pro AI) | Caption Variants (Long text, one per platform) | Status (Single select: Brief, Draft, Approved, In Production, Published) | AI Log ID (Link to AI Log table)

5.6 Repurposing Engine
A single approved long-form piece can be broken into up to 8 derivative assets by Nemotron in a single inference pass:
Asset 1: Full script (original)
Asset 2: 60-second short-form hook cut
Asset 3: LinkedIn long-form article adaptation
Asset 4: Instagram carousel outline (5-7 slides)
Asset 5: Facebook post (short form, 150 words)
Asset 6: Email newsletter segment
Asset 7: Podcast talking point expansion
Asset 8: YouTube description and chapter breakdown
