Investment Acquisition Due Diligence Framework

35 Elite Questions Across 5 Domains — Buy-Side & Sell-Side Positioning
v1.0 — CK-INV-001
Dual Positioning: Each question serves both the buy-side (acquirer evaluating a target) and the sell-side (owner preparing for exit). Buy-side: use red/green flags to score risk. Sell-side: ensure your answers land in the green before going to market.

Section 1: Financial Integrity

FI-01
What percentage of revenue is recurring vs. one-time? Provide 24-month trend.
Red Flag: <50% recurring; declining trend; heavy reliance on project-based income
Green Flag: >75% recurring; growing MRR; predictable cash flow with low churn
Score 1-5: 5 = 90%+ recurring with <5% monthly churn
FI-02
Provide gross margin and EBITDA margin for each of the last 3 fiscal years.
Red Flag: Declining margins; GM below 40%; EBITDA negative or erratic
Green Flag: GM >60%; EBITDA >20%; steady or improving trajectory
Score 1-5: 5 = GM >65% with EBITDA >25% and upward trend
FI-03
Detail all capital expenditures over $5,000 in the last 24 months. What is the remaining useful life of each asset?
Red Flag: Deferred maintenance; aging equipment; no capex budget
Green Flag: Proactive replacement schedule; documented asset register; funded reserves
Score 1-5: 5 = Full asset register with funded replacement schedule
FI-04
What is the average days sales outstanding (DSO) and what percentage of AR is over 90 days?
Red Flag: DSO >60 days; >15% AR past 90 days; no collections process
Green Flag: DSO <30 days; autopay enrollment >80%; clean AR aging
Score 1-5: 5 = DSO <15 days with >90% autopay
FI-05
Provide a 12-month cash flow forecast. What are the assumptions behind it?
Red Flag: No forecast exists; unrealistic growth assumptions; cash-negative months
Green Flag: Conservative model; stress-tested scenarios; 6+ months runway
Score 1-5: 5 = Board-approved forecast with quarterly variance analysis
FI-06
What is the total debt structure? Include all notes, LOCs, equipment leases, and personal guarantees.
Red Flag: Debt/EBITDA >3x; personal guarantees; balloon payments due within 12 months
Green Flag: Debt-free or <1x EBITDA; no personal guarantees; long-term fixed rates
Score 1-5: 5 = Zero debt with fully funded operations
FI-07
What is the customer lifetime value (LTV) and customer acquisition cost (CAC)? What is the LTV:CAC ratio?
Red Flag: LTV:CAC <3:1; rising CAC; no tracking system
Green Flag: LTV:CAC >5:1; declining CAC; segmented by channel
Score 1-5: 5 = LTV:CAC >7:1 with channel-level attribution

Section 2: Operational Risk

OR-01
If the founder/CEO were removed tomorrow, how long before operations degrade? Who assumes command?
Red Flag: Single point of failure; no documented SOPs; tribal knowledge
Green Flag: Documented succession plan; ops manual >200 pages; trained #2
Score 1-5: 5 = Operations run 90+ days without founder intervention
OR-02
What percentage of revenue depends on the top 3 vendors? Are contracts transferable on acquisition?
Red Flag: >50% single-vendor; verbal agreements; non-transferable contracts
Green Flag: No vendor >20%; written contracts; assignment clauses included
Score 1-5: 5 = Fully diversified with transferable multi-year contracts
OR-03
Detail all insurance coverage: GL, E&O, workers comp, cyber, umbrella. When were policies last reviewed?
Red Flag: Gaps in coverage; no E&O; policies expired or under-limit
Green Flag: Comprehensive coverage; annual broker review; umbrella >$2M
Score 1-5: 5 = Full coverage suite reviewed within 90 days
OR-04
List all regulatory licenses, permits, and certifications. Which expire within 12 months?
Red Flag: Missing licenses; expired permits; no renewal calendar
Green Flag: All current; automated renewal alerts; compliance officer assigned
Score 1-5: 5 = 100% current with 90-day advance renewal system
OR-05
What is the average employee tenure? What is annual turnover? What does onboarding cost per hire?
Red Flag: >40% annual turnover; avg tenure <1 year; no training program
Green Flag: <15% turnover; avg tenure >3 years; documented onboarding
Score 1-5: 5 = <10% turnover with certified training pipeline
OR-06
What technology systems run the business? What is the estimated technical debt? When was the last security audit?
Red Flag: Spreadsheet-dependent; no backups; no security audit ever
Green Flag: Modern stack; automated backups; annual pen test; SOC 2 path
Score 1-5: 5 = Cloud-native with automated CI/CD and annual security audit
OR-07
Are all operating procedures documented? Can a new hire execute core workflows from documentation alone?
Red Flag: No SOPs; processes live in people's heads; training is shadowing
Green Flag: Complete SOP library; video training; tested with new hires
Score 1-5: 5 = ISO-grade documentation with quarterly review cycle

Section 3: Market Position

MP-01
What is your defensible competitive moat? How long would it take a well-funded competitor to replicate it?
Red Flag: No moat; commodity service; easily replicated
Green Flag: Technology IP; exclusive contracts; brand equity; network effects
Score 1-5: 5 = Multiple reinforcing moats with 3+ year replication barrier
MP-02
What is your market share in your primary service area? How is it measured?
Red Flag: Unknown market share; no competitive tracking; shrinking position
Green Flag: Quantified share with growth trajectory; competitive intelligence system
Score 1-5: 5 = Dominant position (>15%) with systematic tracking
MP-03
What percentage of revenue comes from your top 5 clients? What happens if you lose the largest?
Red Flag: Top client >25% of revenue; no contracts; no replacement pipeline
Green Flag: No client >5%; long-term agreements; diversified base
Score 1-5: 5 = No client exceeds 3% of revenue
MP-04
When did you last raise prices? What was the client retention impact? What is your pricing power?
Red Flag: Haven't raised in 3+ years; lost clients on last increase; price-sensitive base
Green Flag: Annual increases accepted; <2% churn on increases; premium positioning
Score 1-5: 5 = Annual 5-8% increases with zero churn impact
MP-05
What is your Net Promoter Score? How do you measure and act on client satisfaction?
Red Flag: No NPS tracking; no feedback system; reactive to complaints
Green Flag: NPS >50; systematic surveys; closed-loop feedback process
Score 1-5: 5 = NPS >70 with quarterly measurement and action plan
MP-06
What adjacent markets or geographies could you expand into? What would it cost?
Red Flag: No expansion plan; saturated market; regulatory barriers
Green Flag: Identified 3+ markets; proven expansion playbook; capital allocated
Score 1-5: 5 = Proven expansion model with unit economics validated in 2+ markets
MP-07
What is the total addressable market (TAM) and serviceable addressable market (SAM) for your core offering?
Red Flag: TAM undefined; shrinking market; no growth data
Green Flag: TAM >$500M; growing 10%+ annually; SAM well-defined with penetration plan
Score 1-5: 5 = TAM >$1B with <5% current penetration and clear path to 15%

Section 4: Scalability

SC-01
What are the unit economics at current scale? Model them at 2x, 5x, and 10x. Where do they break?
Red Flag: Negative unit economics; margins compress at scale; no model exists
Green Flag: Positive at all scales; margins improve with volume; model stress-tested
Score 1-5: 5 = Improving unit economics at every scale with validated model
SC-02
Can your technology infrastructure handle 10x current volume without re-architecture?
Red Flag: Single server; manual processes; no monitoring; will crash at 3x
Green Flag: Cloud-native; auto-scaling; load-tested; observability in place
Score 1-5: 5 = Proven at 10x load with zero downtime during scale events
SC-03
Do you have management depth beyond the founder? Who runs operations when leadership is unavailable?
Red Flag: Founder does everything; no middle management; no delegation
Green Flag: Experienced ops manager; department leads; founder is strategic only
Score 1-5: 5 = Full C-suite with proven track record independent of founder
SC-04
What capital is required to double the business? What is the expected payback period?
Red Flag: >$2M required; >36 month payback; capital-intensive model
Green Flag: <$500K; <12 month payback; capital-light with operating leverage
Score 1-5: 5 = Self-funded growth with <6 month payback on expansion capital
SC-05
Is the business model franchise-replicable? What documentation and systems exist for replication?
Red Flag: Not replicable; depends on local relationships; no playbook
Green Flag: Complete franchise kit; technology platform; training certification program
Score 1-5: 5 = FDD-ready with pilot franchise operational
SC-06
What is the operating leverage ratio? How does headcount scale relative to revenue?
Red Flag: Linear headcount:revenue; no automation; labor-intensive
Green Flag: Revenue grows 3x faster than headcount; AI/automation drives leverage
Score 1-5: 5 = Revenue scales 5x+ relative to headcount growth
SC-07
What is your talent pipeline? How quickly can you hire and onboard to meet growth demands?
Red Flag: No pipeline; 60+ days to hire; no training program; key roles unfilled
Green Flag: Active pipeline; <21 day hire; certified training; bench strength
Score 1-5: 5 = Waiting list of candidates; 14-day onboard; zero critical vacancies

Section 5: Exit Strategy

ES-01
Who are the likely buyers? Strategic acquirers, PE firms, family offices, or management buyout?
Red Flag: No identified buyers; niche too small; no broker engaged
Green Flag: 5+ identified strategic buyers; active PE interest; multiple exit paths
Score 1-5: 5 = Active conversations with 3+ qualified buyers
ES-02
What valuation methodology applies? What are comparable transaction multiples in your sector?
Red Flag: No comps available; unrealistic expectations; no third-party valuation
Green Flag: 3+ recent comps; independent valuation; realistic multiple range
Score 1-5: 5 = Recent QoE completed with validated 6-8x EBITDA multiple
ES-03
What is the transition risk? How dependent is the business on the current owner during handoff?
Red Flag: Business is the owner; client relationships personal; 12+ month transition
Green Flag: Institutional relationships; 90-day transition plan; team retains clients
Score 1-5: 5 = Seamless 60-day transition with zero client attrition
ES-04
What earn-out structure would you accept? What metrics would you tie it to?
Red Flag: >50% earn-out; subjective metrics; no control post-close
Green Flag: <20% earn-out; objective revenue/EBITDA targets; accelerators included
Score 1-5: 5 = All-cash at close; no earn-out required
ES-05
What intellectual property exists? Is it protected? How does it transfer on sale?
Red Flag: No IP protection; trade secrets undocumented; employee IP not assigned
Green Flag: Trademarks registered; software copyrighted; IP assignment agreements signed
Score 1-5: 5 = Full IP portfolio with registered protections and clean chain of title
ES-06
Are there any pending or threatened legal claims, disputes, or regulatory actions?
Red Flag: Active litigation; regulatory investigation; unresolved disputes
Green Flag: Clean legal history; no pending claims; rep & warranty insurance available
Score 1-5: 5 = Zero legal exposure with clean diligence report
ES-07
What is the tax structure? Are there any tax liabilities, carryforwards, or structural issues that affect sale?
Red Flag: Back taxes owed; complex entity structure; S-corp with built-in gains
Green Flag: Clean returns; simple structure; QSBS eligible; tax advisor engaged
Score 1-5: 5 = Optimized structure with QSBS exclusion and clean 3-year returns
