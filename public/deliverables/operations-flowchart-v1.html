IV. Scalability Logic

Phase 1 — Startup
1 – 50 Properties
Owner-operator model. CEO handles sales + oversight. 1 field tech. AI handles scheduling, reporting, and CRM. Monthly revenue target: $14,750.
Phase 2 — Growth
50 – 150 Properties
Hire Operations Manager. Add 2 field techs. Zone-based routing (North / South Treasure Coast). CEO shifts to strategy + investor relations. Monthly revenue target: $44,250.
Phase 3 — Scale
150 – 300 Properties
Full team: Ops Manager + 4 techs + office coordinator. Dedicated QA role. AI-first operations with human exception handling. Monthly revenue target: $88,500.
Phase 4 — Enterprise
300+ Properties
Franchise model activated. Regional managers per territory. Technology platform licensed to franchisees. Corporate oversight via Command Center dashboard. Monthly revenue target: $88,500+ per territory.
