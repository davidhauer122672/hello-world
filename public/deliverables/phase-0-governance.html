III. Operating Principles

01
Ship, Then Iterate
V1 ships fast. V2 ships better. Perfection is the enemy of deployment. Every deliverable gets a version number.
02
Measure or Kill
If it can't be measured, it doesn't exist. Every initiative has a KPI. Miss the target 3 cycles in a row — it dies.
03
Zero Silos
Every system feeds every other system. Retail ops connect to the flowchart. The flowchart connects to staffing. Staffing connects to the financial model.
04
Stress Test Everything
If it hasn't broken, it hasn't been tested. Apply maximum pressure before deployment. Fragile systems fail in production.
05
Automate the Repeatable
If a human does it twice, an AI does it forever. Manual processes are bugs, not features.
06
Fiduciary Standard
Every property managed as if owned by the operator. Audit-ready records. Full chain of custody. No exceptions.
