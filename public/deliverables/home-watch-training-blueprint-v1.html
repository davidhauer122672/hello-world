V. Omnichannel Integration

Point of Sale
Stripe + Airtable

Stripe handles recurring billing (autopay enrollment target: 90%). Airtable tracks service history, payment status, and client lifecycle. Webhook sync keeps both systems in real-time alignment.

E-Commerce
coastalkey-pm.com

Online booking via /book.html. Service tier selection with Stripe Checkout. Automated confirmation emails. Client portal for report access (roadmap: Q3 2026).

CRM Pipeline
Airtable 39-Table Architecture

Lead Contacts → Leads → Clients → Inspections → Reports. Full lifecycle tracking. Sentinel AI auto-populates from outbound calls. Manual entry for referrals and walk-ins.

Communication Stack
Email + SMS + Slack

Nodemailer for transactional emails. Twilio for SMS alerts and daily reports. Slack for internal ops (12 programmatic channels). 90-day drip nurture engine for unconverted leads.

VI. Launch Plan

Phase A: Soft Launch (Weeks 1-4)

  • Week 1: Onboard first 5 clients from personal network. Test full inspection → report → billing cycle.
  • Week 2: Activate Sentinel AI fleet (10 agents initially). Begin outbound calls to Stuart/Palm City.
  • Week 3: First QA audit. Refine inspection checklist based on field feedback. Adjust route optimization.
  • Week 4: Review metrics: lead-to-close time, inspection completion rate, client satisfaction. Iterate.

Phase B: Hard Launch (Weeks 5-12)

  • Week 5: Scale Sentinel to full 40-agent fleet. Expand to Jensen Beach and Hobe Sound.
  • Week 6-8: Marketing push: social media campaign (SCM division), Google Business Profile optimization, NHWA directory listing.
  • Week 9-10: Hire first field technician. Implement zone-based routing.
  • Week 11-12: Monthly business review. Adjust pricing if needed. Target: 25 active properties by Week 12.
