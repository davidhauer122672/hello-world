David Hauer, Founder and CEO.

Biography

David Hauer founded Coastal Key Property Management because the Treasure Coast deserved better than what was available. Seasonal homeowners with high-value properties were entrusting them to operators running on handshakes, inconsistent inspections, and no documented standards. The gap between what owners needed and what the market delivered was not a matter of degree. It was structural.

David built Coastal Key from first principles. He studied the failure modes that damage unoccupied properties in coastal Florida: hurricane exposure, sustained humidity, plumbing failures from disuse, pest intrusion through neglected seals, and vendor work performed without oversight. He then engineered an inspection methodology, the Sentinel Standard, that addresses each failure mode through a forty-seven-checkpoint protocol executed on every visit. The standard is not a guideline. It is the minimum deliverable for every property under Coastal Key's care.

David holds accredited membership in the National Home Watch Association, the professional body that establishes ethical and operational standards for home watch providers across the United States. NHWA accreditation requires background checks, proof of insurance, and adherence to a professional code of conduct. Coastal Key maintains two million dollars in general liability coverage and is fully licensed in the State of Florida.

Beyond the inspection standard, David established the company's Sovereign Governance doctrine, a published financial and operational framework that governs every decision at Coastal Key. The doctrine enforces margin discipline with a target EBITDA band of twenty-two to twenty-eight percent, mandates a recurring revenue mix of sixty percent or more, caps client concentration at fifteen percent, and prohibits discounting under any circumstances. These constraints exist to ensure the company remains financially durable and operationally consistent through hurricane seasons, economic cycles, and growth transitions.

David also invested early in technology as an operational multiplier. Coastal Key's platform integrates AI-assisted scheduling, automated photographic reporting, vendor coordination workflows, and a client-facing owner portal that delivers inspection results within twenty-four hours. The technology exists to extend the reach of a lean, high-caliber team rather than to replace human judgment where it matters most.

In his own words

I started Coastal Key because I saw what happens when property management is treated as a commodity. Corners get cut. Reports become vague. Problems compound for months before anyone notices. I built this company to be the opposite of that. Every visit follows the same forty-seven-checkpoint protocol. Every report includes timestamped photographs. Every owner gets the same standard of care regardless of the size of their property or how long they have been a client.

The operating doctrine is public because I believe transparency is the foundation of trust. We publish our financial targets, our pricing, and our governance framework. We do not discount. We do not negotiate rates. The service is the service and the price is the price. That clarity protects our clients because it means the person who signed up last month receives exactly the same attention as the person who has been with us since day one.

My goal is to build the most operationally disciplined home watch and residential asset management firm on the Treasure Coast. Not the biggest. Not the cheapest. The most reliable. If your property is worth protecting, it deserves a standard that does not bend.
