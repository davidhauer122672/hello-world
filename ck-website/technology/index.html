Operating system for your home.

Introduction

Most property management companies run on spreadsheets, text messages, and memory. Coastal Key runs on a purpose-built technology platform that treats your home like the high-value asset it is. Every inspection, every vendor interaction, every report, and every payment flows through a single system designed to protect your property and keep you informed without requiring your constant attention.

The Treasure Coast draws thousands of seasonal residents every year, and the gap between what homeowners need and what the industry delivers has always been operational. Not intentions. Operations. Coastal Key was built from day one around the conviction that property management is a technology problem disguised as a service problem. The platform behind our service handles scheduling, documentation, vendor coordination, client communication, payments, and compliance so that the human team can focus entirely on the work that requires expertise, judgment, and physical presence at your property.

Owner portal

Every Coastal Key client receives access to a secure owner portal where inspection reports, photographs, maintenance records, and service history are available around the clock. When an inspector completes a Sentinel Standard visit, the forty-seven-checkpoint report with timestamped photographs is published to your portal within twenty-four hours. You can review individual inspections, compare photographs across visits to track changes over time, and see a running log of every vendor interaction and maintenance action taken on your property.

The portal is also your primary communication channel with Coastal Key. Service requests, schedule changes, and questions are submitted through the portal and tracked to resolution. You always know where a request stands. Nothing gets lost in a text thread or buried in an email inbox. The portal is accessible from any device, desktop or mobile, with no app to install.

Intelligent operations

Behind the scenes, Coastal Key operates a fleet of more than 250 AI agents organized across fifteen departments. These are not chatbots on a website. They are specialized digital workers that handle the operational volume that would otherwise require a staff five times our size. For you as a homeowner, the result is simple: things happen faster, nothing falls through the cracks, and every detail is documented.

Scheduling and routing. When your inspection is due, our operations agents optimize the route, confirm the appointment window, and prepare the inspector with your property's full history and any outstanding follow-up items from previous visits. You do not need to call to confirm. The system handles it.

Reporting and documentation. After every visit, AI agents process the inspector's photographs and notes, compile them against the forty-seven-checkpoint standard, flag anomalies by comparing against your property's baseline, and publish the finished report to your owner portal. The twenty-four-hour turnaround is not aspirational. It is automated.

Vendor management. When an inspection reveals an issue that needs repair, our vendor coordination agents identify the right contractor from our vetted network, check availability, request quotes, and schedule the work. You receive a summary with the recommended vendor, scope of work, and cost estimate before any work begins. Every vendor interaction is logged in your property record.

Communications. Seasonal preparation checklists, insurance documentation reminders, storm advisories, and market intelligence relevant to your property are generated and delivered automatically. Our financial operations agents process invoices, reconcile vendor payments, and produce the monthly account summaries available in your portal. When a hurricane watch is issued for the Treasure Coast, our intelligence agents monitor weather data and automatically trigger preparation protocols for every property in the projected path.

The result is that a lean human team, each person an expert in their role, is supported by a digital workforce that handles scheduling, data processing, vendor communication, and reporting at a pace and consistency that manual operations cannot match. You experience this as faster response times, more thorough reports, and a property management service that never forgets a follow-up.

ELIZA Concierge

ELIZA is Coastal Key's AI-powered concierge. When you call our main line outside of business hours, ELIZA answers. She can provide a status update on your most recent inspection, schedule a callback with David, log a service request, and escalate urgent issues to our emergency response protocol. ELIZA is trained on Coastal Key's operations, services, and property data so the information she provides is specific to your account, not generic.

During business hours, ELIZA works alongside the human team. She handles initial call routing, appointment scheduling, and routine inquiries so that David and the inspection team can focus on work that requires their expertise. ELIZA is not a replacement for human contact. She is the first layer of response that ensures no call goes unanswered and no request is lost, day or night.

Integrated payments and contracts

Coastal Key uses Stripe for payment processing and DocuSign for contract execution. Monthly subscription payments are processed automatically through your credit card or ACH on file. You receive a receipt for every transaction, and your complete payment history is available in the owner portal at any time.

Service agreements are executed through DocuSign, providing a secure digital signature process with a complete audit trail. When we amend a service agreement, add a seasonal preparation addendum, or update vendor authorization forms, the documents are routed through DocuSign so that everything is signed, timestamped, and stored in your account record. No paper. No fax. No scanning.

These integrations are not add-ons bolted onto a legacy system. They are part of the core operating platform. Every client interaction, from the first consultation booking through years of ongoing service, flows through a single system that keeps your property data, inspection history, financial records, and communication log in one place. That integration is what allows us to deliver the twenty-four-hour reporting commitment, the real-time portal access, and the operational consistency that defines the Coastal Key experience.

Security and privacy

Your property data, photographs, access codes, and personal information are protected by enterprise-grade security controls. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Access to property records is restricted to authorized personnel on a need-to-know basis. Our platform runs on Cloudflare's global edge network with built-in DDoS protection, rate limiting, and content security policies. We do not sell, share, or monetize your data. Your information exists to serve you, and only you.
