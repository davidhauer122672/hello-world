How we work

Coastal Key is built on a doctrine: margin first, recurring revenue second, growth third. We do not cut corners. We do not discount. We deliver institutional-grade service to every property we manage, every visit, every time.

That standard applies to the people who deliver it. We hire carefully, train thoroughly, and hold ourselves to the same Sentinel Standard we apply to our inspections. If you believe in doing the work right -- not fast, not cheap, but right -- this is the kind of firm where you belong.

What we value

  • Accountability. Every visit produces a photographic record. Every task has an owner. We do not hide behind process -- we own outcomes.
  • Precision. Forty-seven checkpoints per inspection. Timestamped documentation. Same-day reporting. The standard is not negotiable.
  • Integrity. NHWA accredited, Florida licensed, fully insured. We operate transparently with published pricing and published doctrine.
  • Autonomy. We trust trained people to make decisions in the field. Sovereign Governance means authority flows to the person closest to the problem.
  • Growth. The firm is growing. As the Treasure Coast portfolio expands, so do opportunities for the people who deliver on our standard.
