How engagement works.

Every Coastal Key engagement follows the same five-step process. No shortcuts. No improvisation. The process is designed so that by the time we conduct your first Sentinel Standard inspection, we already understand your property, your priorities, and the specific risks that need to be monitored.

1. Initial consultation

The engagement begins with a thirty-minute conversation, either by phone or in person, at no cost and with no obligation. We discuss your property, how it is used, how long it sits unoccupied, any known maintenance concerns, and what you expect from a home watch provider. This conversation also covers your communication preferences, whether you want detailed reports after every visit or a summary at the end of each month, and how you want to be notified about urgent issues. The consultation is conducted by David Hauer directly. There is no sales team and no call center. The person you speak with is the person who runs the company.

2. Property assessment

After the consultation, we schedule an on-site property assessment. This is a comprehensive walkthrough of the home covering every area that falls under the Sentinel Standard: exterior envelope, roof condition, landscaping and drainage, interior climate control, plumbing systems, electrical panel and surge protection, security hardware, and access points. We photograph the baseline condition of each area so that future inspections can be compared against a documented starting state. The assessment typically takes sixty to ninety minutes depending on the size and complexity of the property. We also identify any immediate maintenance items that should be addressed before regular service begins.

3. Service agreement

Based on the consultation and the property assessment, we recommend a service tier: Standard Watch for bi-weekly inspections, Premium Concierge for weekly inspections with vendor coordination and emergency response, or Full Asset Management for a bespoke schedule with comprehensive oversight. Pricing is published and non-negotiable. The service agreement is a straightforward monthly subscription with clear terms, no long-term lock-in, and a thirty-day cancellation provision. We execute agreements through DocuSign for convenience and audit trail. Payment is processed monthly through Stripe via credit card or ACH.

4. Onboarding and first inspection

Once the agreement is signed, we configure your property in our operations platform. This includes setting up your owner portal account, programming the inspection schedule, registering key and access information, and documenting your preferred vendors and emergency contacts. Within the first week, we conduct the initial Sentinel Standard inspection, the full forty-seven-checkpoint protocol, and deliver the report through your owner portal with timestamped photographs of every checkpoint. This first report establishes the documented baseline against which all future inspections are measured.

5. Ongoing reporting

From this point forward, every inspection follows the Sentinel Standard and produces a photographic report delivered through your owner portal within twenty-four hours. You can review reports at any time, compare photographs across visits, and track the condition of your property over months and years. If an inspection reveals an issue that requires attention, we flag it immediately with a priority classification and, if you are a Premium Concierge or Full Asset Management client, coordinate the vendor response on your behalf. You receive a monthly summary that consolidates all inspection findings, completed maintenance, and upcoming recommendations. The reporting never stops and the standard never bends.
