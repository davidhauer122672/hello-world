# Retail Shoe & Apparel Business Blueprint
**Version**: 1.0.0 | **Standard**: SpaceX Engineering Logic

## Brand: Premium-accessible athletic/lifestyle, $65-$220 footwear, $25-$120 apparel
Palette: Charcoal #2D2D2D, Warm White #F5F0EB, Accent Gold #C4A265
Voice: Confident, knowledgeable, never desperate

## Product Mix
- Core Footwear 35% | $120 avg | 50% margin | 4x turn
- Trend Footwear 15% | $160 avg | 55% margin | 6x turn
- Core Apparel 25% | $55 avg | 55% margin | 5x turn
- Trend Apparel 10% | $85 avg | 60% margin | 6x turn
- Accessories 10% | $25 avg | 65% margin | 8x turn
- Impulse 5% | $12 avg | 70% margin | 10x turn

## Financials
- Startup: $135K (lease $12K, build-out $25K, inventory $45K, working capital $27K)
- Monthly OpEx: $19K (rent $2.5K, payroll $5.5K, inventory $8K, marketing $1.5K)
- Break-even: Month 6 at $38K revenue ($475/sq ft annualized)
- Year 1: $380-420K revenue, $15-30K net (reinvest)
- Gross margin: 52% | Net margin at scale: ~23%

## Store Layout (1,200 sq ft)
- Entry/Feature Wall 150 sqft: New arrivals, rotate biweekly
- Right Wall (Footwear) 300 sqft: Best product, 90% turn right
- Center Floor (Apparel) 350 sqft: 1 rack per 50 sqft max
- Left Wall (Impulse) 100 sqft: Counter display, +$18/transaction
- Try-On Area 150 sqft: Sit down = 60% close rate jump
- Back Stock 150 sqft: Not customer-facing

## Omnichannel: Shopify POS + ecomm (single inventory pool)
## Launch: Soft (weeks 1-2, reduced hours) then Hard (week 3, full + event)
## Acquisition: $1,500/mo budget, $11 avg CAC, ~195 new customers/mo
## Metrics: Revenue/sqft $350+, conversion 25%, avg ticket $85, turn 5x, labor 6:1
