### Entity Rationale

The parent company, Coastal Key R.E.S. Intelligence Co., exists as the intellectual property and technology holder. All proprietary AI systems, the 383-agent autonomous fleet, the 149-endpoint API gateway, the 39-table Airtable infrastructure, and the sovereign governance framework are owned at the parent level. This structure enables:

1. **IP Protection.** Software assets held separately from operating risk.
2. **Franchise Licensing.** Technology licensed downstream to franchise units at royalty rates.
3. **Tax Efficiency.** IP licensing fees create legitimate intercompany transfer pricing.
4. **Acquisition Readiness.** Clean separation of technology from operations for LBO or strategic sale.
5. **Social Impact Isolation.** Foundation operates as a separate entity with its own governance.
