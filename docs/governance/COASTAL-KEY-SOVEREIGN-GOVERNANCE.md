# SECTION B: SOVEREIGN INSTITUTIONAL GOVERNANCE

## IV. Governance Principles

The following 10 principles govern all Coastal Key operations. They are non-negotiable.

1. **Home Watch and Asset Management operate under unified governance.** No operational silo.
2. **Margin discipline governs all strategic decisions.** Growth never at the expense of margin.
3. **Recurring revenue remains the primary enterprise driver.** Every new product must generate MRR.
4. **Risk controls are documented and audited.** No undocumented operational risk.
5. **Board oversight is active and recorded.** Quarterly board reviews with written minutes.
6. **Capital allocation requires written CEO approval.** No spend above $500 without authorization.
7. **Client retention is monitored monthly.** Churn prediction AI (analysis suite) flags at-risk clients 60 days before renewal.
8. **Compliance logs are archived seven years minimum.** All audit trails, financial records, and regulatory filings.
9. **AI intelligence supports but does not replace human authority.** The CEO is the sovereign. AI agents execute. The CEO decides.
10. **All operations must be audit-ready at all times.** Any investor, regulator, or acquirer can inspect at any moment.
