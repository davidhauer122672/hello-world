## CONVERSATION FLOW SCRIPT (3-Minute Interactive Dialog)

### PHASE 1: Opening (0:00 - 0:30) — Warm Introduction

**Agent:**
"Hi, good [morning/afternoon]! Is this [PROSPECT_NAME]?"

*[Wait for confirmation]*

"Great! My name is [AGENT_NAME], and I'm reaching out on behalf of Tracey Hunter with RE/MAX of Stuart. I'm not calling to sell you anything today — I just wanted to connect briefly because we've been working with homeowners in the [PROSPECT_AREA] area, and I had a quick question for you. Do you have just a couple of minutes?"

*[If YES → Continue to Phase 2]*
*[If NO → "I completely understand. Would there be a better time for a quick two-minute chat? I promise to be respectful of your time."]*
*[If HARD NO → "No problem at all. I appreciate your time. Have a wonderful day!"]*

---

### PHASE 2: Discovery & Engagement (0:30 - 1:30) — Qualifying Questions

**Agent:**
"Perfect, I appreciate that. So [PROSPECT_NAME], I'm curious — with everything happening in the real estate market right now on the Treasure Coast, have you given any thought to either buying or selling a home in the near future?"

#### SELLER PATH:
*[If they mention selling or seem open]*

"That's great to hear. What's prompting you to think about making a move? Is it something like relocating, downsizing, or maybe just taking advantage of the current market?"

*[Listen and respond naturally]*

"That makes total sense. And just so I can give Tracey the right picture — do you have a general idea of what your home might be worth in today's market?"

*[Listen]*

"Got it. And what kind of timeline are you thinking — is this something you'd want to move on in the next few months, or more of a longer-term plan?"

#### BUYER PATH:
*[If they mention buying]*

"Oh, that's exciting! What kind of property are you looking for? Are you focused on a particular area — maybe Stuart, Palm City, Jensen Beach, or the Palm Beach area?"

*[Listen and respond naturally]*

"Great taste in areas. And are you looking to make a move pretty soon, or are you still in the early stages of exploring?"

*[Listen]*

"And do you have a general price range in mind, or would you like some guidance on what's available in your target area?"

#### NEITHER PATH:
*[If they say no to both]*

"Totally fair. Sometimes people aren't actively looking but are curious about what their home is worth in this market. Would you be interested in getting a free, no-obligation home value report? It only takes about 30 seconds to set up, and there's absolutely no commitment."

*[If YES → Collect property address and email, note as nurture lead]*
*[If NO → "I understand completely. Well, if anything changes down the road, Tracey Hunter is one of the top agents in Florida — a Platinum RE/MAX associate — and she'd love to be a resource for you. Can I leave her contact info with you just in case?"]*

| "How did you get my number?" | "Great question. Your information was available through public property records. We reach out to homeowners in the area to offer market insights and see if we can be helpful." |

| "Send me information" | "Absolutely! What's the best email address for you? I'll have Tracey send over some relevant market info. And her website, traceyhuntergroup.com, has some great resources too." |
