## Property Management

**How much does property management cost?**
Our management fees are competitive and transparent. We'd be happy to provide a customized quote based on your property type and needs. There are no hidden fees — everything is outlined upfront in our management agreement.

**How do you screen tenants?**
We run comprehensive checks: credit report, criminal background, rental history verification, employment and income verification, and previous landlord references. We only place tenants who meet our qualification standards.

**How do you handle maintenance?**
Tenants submit requests through our system. We coordinate with our vetted vendor network, get competitive bids for larger jobs, and keep owners informed throughout the process. We handle emergency calls 24/7.

**Do you manage short-term rentals?**
Yes. We offer full STR management including listing optimization on Airbnb, VRBO, and Booking.com, dynamic pricing, guest communication, professional cleaning coordination, and review management.

**What about seasonal or snowbird properties?**
Our Seasonal Care Program covers winterization and opening, regular property checks, mail management, landscape and pool oversight, storm preparation, and guest coordination when your family visits.

**How do I submit a maintenance request?**
Current tenants can call us at 772-763-8900 or submit requests through our online portal. For emergencies (flooding, no heat/AC, security issues), call us immediately — we handle emergencies 24/7.
